Lease Term

One year term with possibility of extension.

Security Deposit

The security deposit is equal in value to one month's rent, due at move-in. If interested, please request a rental application. Each individual over 18 years of age interested in this apartment must complete a rental application. There is a non-refundable $25 per person fee to process each application, which covers a credit score check.
Qualified applicants must provide proof of income for the last two months. Monthly income should be at least three times the monthly rent.

Renter's Insurance

Renter's Insurance is mandatory. The policy should cover the tenants' personal property (minimum $5,000), and personal liability (minimum $100,000).

Pet Policy

Only one cat is allowed on the premises. No dogs are allowed, as per community guidelines. Tenants must provide proof of up-to-date vaccination and medication of their cat, their veterinarian's name, and also a picture of the cat. Pet monthly rent is $25. There is a one time non-refundable Pet Convenience Fee of $200 due at move-in.

Parking

Tenant is entitled to one designated parking space for their personal vehicle. Additional public parking spaces are also available.

Utilities

Tenant is financially responsible for the services established by Tenant in connection with the premises, such as electricity, gas, cable, TV, telephone, Internet etc. until the last day of their Term (inclusive). Included in rent is: water, trash, snow removal, common grounds maintenance, parking, monthly condo dues and property taxes.
